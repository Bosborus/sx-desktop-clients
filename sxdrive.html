SXDrive - File sync & share

Keep your data safe

SXDrive safely stores your files in SX Cluster and keeps them synced across all your PCs (Windows, OS X, Linux), mobiles (iOS, Android) and web app.

Multi-platform

Synchronize your files on the go between all your devices and keep them safe with client-side encryption. Available for PCs, mobiles, and as a web app.

Features

Fast

Never transfer the same data twice. SXDrive transfers only the differences between files and parallelizes transfers.

Easy

Store your data on SX Cluster using commodity servers and get replication and high availability without any effort.

Safe

Client side encryption ensures that only the owner of the files have access to them. The sysadmin cannot access the client data.
